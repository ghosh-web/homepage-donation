Della Kaushik

A Give volunteer for the past 7 years, Somejyoti leaves no stone unturned in doing all sorts of different things (including raising funds and even donating a laptop!) to take children closer to their dreams. For the last 5 years, Rajan has been leading beach cleanup initiatives every weekend. His dedication has inspired over 200 regular volunteers to join his cause. After retiring as a teacher, Meera found her second calling in organizing literacy programs for underprivileged adults.
